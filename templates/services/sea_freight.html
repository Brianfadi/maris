Frequently Asked Questions

FCL (Full Container Load) is used when you have enough cargo to fill an entire container, while LCL (Less than Container Load) is used for smaller shipments that don't require a full container.

Shipping times vary depending on the route and service type. Generally, sea freight can take 20-40 days for major routes.

Required documents typically include Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, and any necessary permits or certificates.
